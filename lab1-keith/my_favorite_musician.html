About Bôa

Bôa is an alternative rock band formed in London in 1993 by drummer Ed Herten, keyboardist Paul Turrell, and Guitarist/vocalist Steve Rodgers. The band evolved over time playing funk at first and then to rock after bassist Caird and multi-instrumentalist Ben Henderson joined, and Jasmine Rodgers was added as a vocalist.

Bôa produced two major albums in the 2000s, Twilight (2001) and Get There (2005). Their 1998 single "Duvet" has been certified Platinum by the RIAA. The band released the album Whiplash in 2024, their first new music in 19 years.

Discography

Twilight
A reissued version of the 1998 album, during recording of the album the band members were Alex Caird, Ben Henderson, Jasmine Rodgers, Steve Rodgers, Lee Sullivan, and Paul Turrell. The opening track, "Duvet", became popular after being used as the opening theme song of the 1998 anime television series Serial Experiments Lain.
Whiplash
In June 2023, Bôa announced that they would release a new album titled Whiplash.
